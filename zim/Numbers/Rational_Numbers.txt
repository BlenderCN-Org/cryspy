====== Rational Numbers ======
Created Sonntag 10 April 2016

There exists the package quicktions, which is a C-Rebuild of fractions, and therefore qick.

Usage:

'''
import quicktions as fr
a = fr.Fraction(3, 6)
'''

Then a is the Fraction 1/2.
